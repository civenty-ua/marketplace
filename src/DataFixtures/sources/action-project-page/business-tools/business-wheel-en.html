BUSINESS WHEEL

Ukrainian fruit and vegetable business development project offers its clients a new tool that allows you to quickly analyze the potential economic benefit from a particular culture.

Thanks to the business wheel in a short period of time you have the opportunity to evaluate potential income of 10 acres depending on the yield of the selected crop and the possible price implementation.

This tool is designed for microbusiness representatives who not can determine which culture to pay attention to, and have difficulty calculating economic benefits, - says UHBDP interproject program manager, Alexandra Garmash.

These indicators in the business cycle of productivity are calculated on 10 hundred parts of the earth. If y you 20 acres, respectively, multiply the data by 2.
