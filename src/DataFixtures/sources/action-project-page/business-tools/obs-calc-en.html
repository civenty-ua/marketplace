OBS CALCULATOR

The purpose of the instrument is to keep financial records on farm, recording income, current and capital expenditures to calculate farm profits, cost products, crop profitability. Analyze and look for opportunities to increase profitability.

Development made in Excel. To have instant access to it, including and from the phone, I recommend downloading it to Google tables (save it as Google table, not downloaded Excel file to Google Drive for speed).

The development is made for a farm where potatoes and garlic are grown. However, you can adapt it to your farm, because the logic of development is universal and practical application in various areas of small business.
